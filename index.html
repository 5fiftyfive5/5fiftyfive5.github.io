About

Hello World!
UBG Oasis was created as a sucessor to TBG95, another great unblocked games website. Issue is, our school found the site and decided to block most of the games on there. Weirdly enough, they didn't block the actual site, just the games individually. I decided it's probably a good idea to make my own. I had to completely re-learn web development for this, so I hope you like it! Not all fo the games might work, like Doom or UCN, but most have been tested and seem to be fine. DO NOT switch skins in Subway Surfers! We are pretty sure that completely bricks the game. You can reset all site data in the settings app to fix it.
